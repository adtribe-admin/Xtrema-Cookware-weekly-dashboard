## Referral / partner offers

### "Stronger Together" referral program (launching)

**New-join perk:** $0 joining fee + free fitness/body scan + 50% off first month.

**Existing member tiers:**
- **Local Advocate** (1–2 joins/yr) → 50% off next month + $25 fuel bar credit
- **Local Champion** (3–5 joins) → 100% off next month or 1 free PT + merch + $100 fuel bar credit
- **Local Ambassador** (6+ joins) → 10% rev-share + unlimited membership + content collab budget

### Affiliate program (planned)
Flat A$40 cash per activated join. 6-month targets: 100 active affiliates, 400 joins, A$25k incremental PT revenue, 70% NPS.

## Ad copy quick reference

### Hero services to lead with (copy anchors)
- **24/7 access** — "Train on your time, not ours."
- **Crèche** — "Drop them in. Focus on you. Pick them up, better."
- **Group fitness** — "You'll never train alone."
- **Personal training** — "Locals training locals."
- **Fuel bar** — "Post-workout, without leaving."
- **Sauna + steam** — "Your recovery, included."

### "All-in-one" positioning
One weekly price. No hidden fees. No upsells. No sales pressure. Everything you need, all under one roof.

*(Note: the "no hidden fees" line is the target state — currently undermined by access/joining fees; use with care until product fix lands.)*

## Do not advertise

- Offers not currently live
- "25% off referral" (being retired — too generous, CAC ~A$800)
- Any promo not confirmed with central marketing
- "Got It In One" as a headline until the value-pillar friction (joining/access fees) is resolved

## Tone guardrail for offer copy

Warm, low-pressure, honest about what's included. Never "WARNING", "last chance", "don't miss out". Read `03-brand-voice-guide.md` before writing any offer copy.
