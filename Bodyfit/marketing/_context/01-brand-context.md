# Bodyfit — Brand Context

## What Bodyfit is

Bodyfit is an independent, family-founded Australian gym chain with 8 locations across NSW. It began as a single family-owned gym in Blacktown and has grown into a hyperlocal network offering 24/7 access, group fitness classes, personal training, crèche, fuel bar, and sauna/steam rooms — all under one weekly "all-in-one" membership.

Bodyfit is not a corporate chain. It is positioned as the community gym — "locals training locals" — the independent alternative to Anytime Fitness, Fitness First, and BFT.

Note: Bodyfit is frequently confused with Body Fit Training (BFT) — a different brand. Canonical written form is **Bodyfit**.

## Business model

**Type:** Lead generation (subscription-based gym memberships).

**Primary conversion event:** Free trial sign-up — currently the "2-Day Free Pass" — which feeds into sales/tour flow and paid membership conversion.

**Secondary conversion:** Booking a gym tour / in-person visit.

**Revenue:** Recurring weekly membership payments.

## Audience

**Overall:** Hyperlocal — ~5km is the core proximity driver; 7km is the default ad targeting radius; >10km is wasted spend.

**Demographics:**
- Predominantly Millennial (30s) and Gen Z (20s) mix
- Gender skew varies by location:
  - Shellharbour: 52% female
  - Auburn / Blacktown / Redfern: 60%+ male
  - Glebe / Marrickville / Miranda: most balanced

**Default one-size-fits-all target:** Millennial men within 7km of each gym.

## Current challenges

- Over-reliance on word-of-mouth — digital share too low
- Meta ads misaligned — single TOF campaign running BOF offer, no MOF layer, CPA too high
- Website is a "digital brochure" — no conversion features
- Aggressive sales calling (daily first week, weekly after) — causing negative Google/Reddit reviews
- "Got It In One" tagline conflicts with the Value pillar (because of joining/access fees)
- Referral program underleveraged and too generous
- Data siloed across ClubFit, Salesforce, Meta
- SEO / LLM visibility poor
- App limited to class bookings — no 2-way member comms
- One marketing manager stretched across 8 locations
- Brand confusion with Body Fit Training (BFT)

## Customer lifecycle

1. Lead source: ~65% referrals/walk-ins, ~35% digital
2. Digital lead: 2-Day Free Pass form on web / Instagram
3. Follow-up: currently ≥ 24 hour lag (should be <15 minutes)
4. Current sales pattern: daily call barrage first week, weekly calls after (to be replaced with 1 call + 1 VM + 1 personalised SMS within 15 min)
5. Tour / free trial
6. Conversion to paid member
7. Member management via ClubFit + Salesforce
